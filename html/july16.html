Forest Fires - Hellscape

There are forest fires burning in Canada right now. Which seems to have been a problem for the last few years and I'm not sure what sup with that. I mean I looked it up and they said that the issue has several different factors including, dry condition, lightning strikes, and of course people. The problem is it seems the last few years it has had a pretty notable affect on those of us living in the United States.

Recently the smoke from this has made it's way south to the United States. I think it's more reasonable for states further north to see this, I'm talking like Michigan, New York, etc. You can see it with the naked eye in Pittsburgh, which at least for me, was pretty shocking. Of course I heard about it on the news in the morning, but I didn't expect it to be so apparent.

The really crazy part is that you can go outside and smell the it. Like I said the past few years we haver been hearing about wild fires in Canada and maybe even seen a little smoke as a result. Typically though it's a layer removed, the news tells you the air quality is low, and you may notice a little smog, but you don't see smoke. You definitely don't smell it!

Even my in-laws who live as far south as Hampton Roads VA, which for those who do not know is pretty close to the border of North Carolina, so pretty far south, have said that they can see the smoke.
