Pickelsburgh

I found out today that this coming weekend is going to be Picklesburgh!

I have lived in Pittsburgh for nearly 11 years and I have not been yet. Normally I don't want to deal with the crowds and the heat, and while I expect that to remain true, I am still wanting to go. I don't want to be 70 years old and not able to go and not having ever gone. Also, the world may not exist by the time I'm 70.

I am going to talk to my friend Mike and see if he and his wife want to come with us. Apparently this year its going to be a little different as it is going to span both the Roberto Clemente Bridge and the Andy Warhol bridge.

Earlier this year we were at the Pittsburgh Home and Garden show and I purchased some horse radish pickles. They were mind blowing! I've been trying to find some that good since then, the closest I have come is the sweet heat ones from Aldi. They're good, but not really on the same level. I'm hoping I can find some really good ones there. I don't think I have any interest in trying pickel beer, or any weird other things.
